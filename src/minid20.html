For skill checks and saving throws, roll 1d20 + stat modifier and any bonuses or penalties (from class, race, equipment, etc.).

The skill succeeds if it is equal or greater than a difficulty set by the DM. If you have advantage, roll twice and take the better result; for disadvantage, roll twice and take the worse.
