Choose your equipment

Any weapons, armor, and mundane items (rope, torches, etc.) that befit your character and that you can reasonably carry in battle.

Weapons
Ranged 1d8 - Bow, crossbow, throwing knives.
Small 1d6 - Dagger, hunting knife.
Medium 1d10 - Need +1 STR or disadvantage on attacks. Sword, mace, other one-handed weapons.
Large 1d12 - Need +2 STR or disadvantage on attacks. Can't use w/ shield. Greataxe, warhammer, polearm.
Armor
Unarmored AC: 10 + DEX. Robes, normal clothing.
Light AC: 11 + DEX. Leather, padded armor.
Medium AC: 13 + DEX (max 2). Disadvantage to stealth, can't cast spells. Hide, chainmail.
Heavy AC 16. Disadvantage on all DEX skills and saves, can't cast spells. Disadvantage on attacks unless barbarian, fighter, or paladin. Plate and splint.
Shield +2 AC, -1 to attack rolls. Can't cast spells.

Character advancement

At lvl 1 you start with hitpoints equal to your max hit die (HD) + CON (eg: 8 + CON for 1d8 HD). For each subsequent level, roll your HD and add the result + CON to your total.

At levels 4, 8, 12, 16, and 19, increase one stat of your choice by 1.

Your class gives you a +2 bonus to listed skills at lvl 1, scaling to +3 at 5, +4 at 9, +5 at 13, and +6 at 17.

Combat and recovery

Ranged vs melee (DEX vs STR, cannot use ranged while engaged with any opponent). Rests ("per rest" skills mean "long rest"). Initiative. Movement + action.

If you drop to 0 hitpoints or less, you die.

Skill checks and saving throws.

For skill checks and saving throws, roll 1d20 + stat modifier and any bonuses or penalties (from class, race, equipment, etc.).

STR Athletics, intimidation.
DEX Acrobatics, sleight of hand, stealth.
INT Arcana, history, investigation, nature, religion.
WIS Animal handling, insight, medicine, perception, survival.
CHA Deception, performance, persuasion.

The skill succeeds if it is equal or greater than a difficulty set by the DM. If you have advantage, roll twice and take the better result; for disadvantage, roll twice and take the worse.
